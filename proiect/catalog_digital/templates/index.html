New Approach to Kids Education

The New Approach to Kids Education is a progressive educational philosophy that emphasizes personalized learning and hands-on experiences. In this approach, children are encouraged to take an active role in their own education and are taught to think critically and creatively. The curriculum is designed to be flexible and adaptable, with a focus on real-world problem solving and collaboration. Teachers act as facilitators, guiding students towards their goals and encouraging exploration and experimentation. The New Approach to Kids Education values social and emotional learning as well as academic achievement, helping students develop the skills they need to succeed both in and out of the classroom.

Music and Dance

Arts and Crafts

Our school offers a vibrant arts and crafts program that fosters creativity and self-expression. Through a variety of hands-on projects, students develop their artistic skills and explore different mediums, including paint, clay, fabric, and more.

Safe Transportation

At our school, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our students, including during transportation to and from school. We provide safe and reliable transportation services that are monitored by trained drivers and staff.

Healthy food

At our school, we believe that a healthy diet is essential for the growth and development of our students. That's why we provide nutritious and delicious meals that are prepared on-site using fresh and locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Educational Tour

Our school offers educational tours that provide students with valuable learning experiences outside of the classroom. These tours are carefully curated to align with our curriculum and provide hands-on learning opportunities that enhance classroom learning.
